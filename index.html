A BETTER SHIPPING EXPERIENCE

wynd is an on-demand shipping and logistics company that, via a suite of dashboard, web, and mobile products, optimizes the shipping and post-purchase experience of brands. By leveraging their volumne you get lower rates for better service.

Features

Because of our volumes, we have access to shipping rates that aren’t otherwise available. As a result, you will often spend less shipping with us than you would via a traditional carrier.

SAME-DAY DELIVERY

Shoppers today enjoy a proliferation of shopping opportunities and 1 in 3 customers say speed of delivery is key factor when choosing one marketplace over another. In fact, 1 in 4 urban shoppers expect orders placed before 5 P.M. to still receive same-day delivery. We allow retailers of all sizes to meet these expectations without time consuming and costly investment in drivers and a logistics team. We even allow you to give your customers the ability to choose their delivery windows.

INTEGRATION

In addition to offering integerations with nearly all major e-commerce platforms, we also offer a fully featured API that allows you to create shipments and schedule pickups. We are also happy to discuss any custom integration needs you may have, so shoot us an email at hello@wynd.it to arrange a time that’s convenient for you.

POST-DELIVERY EXPERIENCE

The customer experience doesn’t end when we hand off the package and neither do our services. We offer complete fulfillment solutions and the ability to distinguish yourself with custom boxes, tape, and packaging materials. Our dashboard also allows you to generate return labels that you can easily send your customers should the need arise, in additional to providing you real-time updates on shipment status so you can stay in the loop.
